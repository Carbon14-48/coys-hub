Who has the most goals for Tottenham Hotspur?

Harry Kane holds the all-time record with 280 goals in competitive matches, making him the club’s most prolific scorer ever.

Who has the most assists for Tottenham Hotspur?

With 101 assists Son Heung-min leads the modern-era charts for assists, combining pace, precision, and vision to set up countless goals for his teammates

Who is the fans’ favourite?

Son Heung-min — loved for his skill, smile, and relentless work rate — is adored by Spurs fans around the globe.

Is Tottenham truly a trophyless club?

Not at all. Spurs have a proud history, including two league titles, eight FA Cups, four League Cups, and two European trophies — but they’ve been chasing their next major honour for quite some time.
